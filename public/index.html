Flexible Booking Periods

We offer six booking types to fit every need — from a single day to a full year.

Daily

Book any single day. Minimum booking period — great for testing a space before committing.

Weekend Block

Saturday & Sunday block. Perfect for market days, pop-up events, and weekend vendors.

Weekday Block

Monday through Friday block. Ideal for professionals, consultants, and service businesses.

Weekly

7 consecutive days from your chosen start date. Great for short-term residencies or launches.

Monthly

A full calendar month. Best value for regular vendors establishing a consistent presence.
